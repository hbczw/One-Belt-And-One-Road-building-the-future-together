The "five" content of the Belt and Road strategy

（一）Strengthen policy communication between countries

Strengthen intergovernmental cooperation, and actively build a multi-level intergovernmental macro-policy communication and exchange mechanism, deepen the integration of interests, promote political mutual trust and reach a new consensus on cooperation. Countries along the country can be on the economic development strategies and countermeasures to fully exchange docking, jointly develop regional cooperation planning and measures to discuss the problems in cooperation, for the pragmatic cooperation and large-scale project implementation to provide policy support.

"the Belt and Road" strategy will strengthen the policy of communication between countries as one of the main content is to improve the slightly tired of the international policy communication mechanism to contribute and demonstrate. On the basis of political mutual trust, for the macroeconomic policy, development strategy and regional planning in three areas, the formation of more pragmatic cooperation, coordination and effective policy support mechanism.

（二）To achieve inter-state facilities Unicom

First, to seize the key infrastructure of the transport infrastructure, key nodes and key projects, give priority to open the missing sections, smooth bottlenecks, supporting the improvement of road safety facilities and traffic management facilities and equipment to enhance the road access level. To promote the establishment of a unified mechanism for the whole transport coordination to promote international customs clearance, facelift, multimodal transport organic convergence, and gradually form a compatible regulatory rules of transport to achieve international transport facilitation. Promote the construction of port infrastructure, smooth land and water transport channels, promote the construction of port cooperation, increase maritime routes and frequency, strengthen the maritime logistics information cooperation. Expand the platform and mechanism for the establishment of civil aviation comprehensive cooperation, and accelerate the upgrading of aviation infrastructure.

Second, strengthen the energy infrastructure interconnection and cooperation, and jointly maintain the transportation of oil and gas pipelines and other transportation channels to promote cross-border power and transmission channel construction, and actively carry out regional power grid upgrade cooperation.

Third, jointly promote cross-border cable and other communications trunk network construction, improve the level of international communication interoperability, smooth information Silk Road. Speed ​​up the construction of bilateral cross-border cable, planning and construction of intercontinental submarine cable project, improve the air (satellite) information channel, expand information exchange and cooperation.

（三）To achieve trade between countries

First, efforts should be made to promote bilateral trade cooperation, such as customs clearance, mutual recognition, mutual legal cooperation, inspection and quarantine, certification and accreditation, standard measurement and statistical information, and promote trade facilitation of the World Trade Organization Agreement "entered into force and implemented. Improve the border conditions of border ports, speed up the border port "single window" construction, reduce customs costs, improve customs clearance capacity. Strengthen supply chain security and facilitate cooperation, promote cross-border regulatory procedures to promote the inspection and quarantine certificate to verify the Internet, to carry out "certified operators" (AEO) mutual recognition. Reduce non-tariff barriers, and jointly improve the transparency of technical trade measures and improve the level of trade liberalization and facilitation.

Second, broaden the scope of trade, optimize the trade structure, the development of cross-border e-commerce and other new business formats. Establish and improve the service trade promotion system, consolidate and expand the traditional trade, vigorously develop the modern service trade. The investment and trade organic combination to invest in trade development.

Third, speed up the process of investment facilitation, to eliminate investment barriers. Strengthen the bilateral investment protection agreement, avoid double taxation agreement consultation, protect the legitimate rights and interests of investors.

Fourth, expand mutual investment areas, to carry out agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry and fishery, agricultural and agricultural production and processing areas such as deep cooperation, and actively promote mariculture, marine engineering technology, environmental protection industry and maritime tourism and other fields of cooperation. Increase cooperation in the exploration and development of traditional energy resources such as coal, oil and gas, metal and mineral resources, and actively promote clean and renewable energy cooperation such as hydropower, nuclear power, wind power and solar energy, and promote the cooperation and transformation of energy resources in the field. Industrial chain. Strengthen the deep processing of energy resources technology, equipment and engineering services.

Fifth, to promote the cooperation of new industries, in accordance with the principle of complementarity, mutual benefit and win-win principle, to promote the countries along the country to strengthen cooperation in the new generation of information technology, biotechnology, new energy, new materials and other emerging industries to promote the establishment of venture capital cooperation mechanism.

Sixth, optimize the industrial chain division of labor, to promote the upstream and downstream industry chain and related industries to develop, encourage the establishment of research and development, production and marketing system to enhance the regional industry supporting capacity and comprehensive competitiveness. Expand the service industry to open up each other, and promote the development of regional service industry.

（四）Actively explore the financing of funds

Expand the scope and scale of bilateral national currency exchange, settlement. To promote the opening and development of the Asian bond market. Jointly promote the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank, the BRICS National Development Bank to build, the parties concerned on the establishment of the Shanghai Cooperation Organization financing institutions to carry out consultations. Speed up the formation of Silk Road Fund operations. Deepen the China - ASEAN Bank Consortium, the SCO consortium in pragmatic cooperation, syndicated loans, bank credit, etc. to carry out multilateral financial cooperation. Support the government along the line and the higher credit rating of enterprises and financial institutions in China issued RMB bonds. Eligible domestic financial institutions and enterprises can issue RMB bonds and foreign currency bonds abroad, encouraging the use of funds in the countries along the line.

At the same time, strengthen financial cooperation between countries to promote the signing of bilateral regulatory cooperation memorandum of understanding, and gradually in the region to establish efficient regulatory coordination mechanism. Improve the risk response and crisis disposal system arrangements, the construction of regional financial risk early warning system, the formation of cross-border risk and crisis response to the exchange and cooperation mechanism. And strengthen cross-border exchanges and cooperation between credit management departments, credit authorities and rating agencies. Give full play to Silk Road funds and the role of national sovereign funds to guide the commercial equity investment funds and social funds to participate in "the Belt and Road" key projects.

（五）To promote communication between the people of different countries

First, to expand the scale of each student, to carry out cooperative education, China each year to the country along the country to provide 10,000 government scholarship places. Along the line between countries to organize cultural years, art festivals, film festivals, TV week and book exhibition and other activities, to carry out radio and television drama boutique creation and translation, joint application of world cultural heritage, joint development of World Heritage joint work. Deepen the exchanges and cooperation between countries along the country.

Second, strengthen tourism cooperation, expand the scale of tourism, mutual tourism tourism week, publicity month and other activities, jointly create a silk with the characteristics of the international boutique tourist routes and tourism products, to improve the level of tourist visa convenience along the level. To promote the 21st century maritime Silk Road cruise tourism cooperation. Actively carry out sports exchange activities, to support countries along the bid to host major international sports events.

Third, strengthen cooperation with neighboring countries in infectious diseases, prevention and control of technical exchanges, professional personnel training and other aspects of cooperation to improve cooperation in dealing with public health emergencies. Strengthen scientific and technological cooperation, build joint laboratories (research center), international technology transfer center, maritime cooperation center, promote scientific and technological personnel exchanges, cooperation in major scientific and technological research, and jointly enhance scientific and technological innovation.

Fourth, give full play to the role of political parties, parliamentary exchanges, strengthen the legislatures along the line between the major parties and political organizations friendly exchanges. Strengthen the exchange and cooperation of national civil society organizations along the line, focusing on grassroots people, extensive education and medical care, poverty alleviation and development, biodiversity and ecological environmental protection and other charitable activities to promote the poor areas along the production and living conditions improved. Strengthen the international exchange and cooperation of cultural media, and actively use the network platform, the use of new media tools, create a harmonious and friendly cultural ecology and public opinion environment.
