  • THE BELT AND ROAD

    What is the "The Belt and Road"?

    "The Belt and Road" is the "Silk Road Economic Zone" and "21st Century Maritime Silk Road" short. It will rely heavily on China and the relevant countries of the existing double multilateral mechanism, with the existing, effective regional cooperation platform, along the way to borrow the ancient Silk Road, historical symbols, hold high the banner of peaceful development, and actively develop and Along the country's economic partnership, together to create political mutual trust, economic integration, cultural inclusion of the interests of the community, the fate of the community and the responsibility of the community.

    The meaning of the Silk Road

    Silk Road, referred to as Silk Road, generally refers to the Silk Road on land, broadly speaking, is divided into the Silk Road and the Silk Road on the sea. On the land Silk Road originated in the Western Han Dynasty (the first 202 years - 8 years) Han Emperor sent Zhang Qian to the Western Regions opened up with the capital Changan (now Xi'an) as a starting point, by Gansu, Xinjiang, to Central Asia, West Asia, and connected to the Mediterranean The landlord of each country, its initial role is to transport silk produced in ancient China. "Maritime Silk Road" is the ancient Chinese and foreign traffic and cultural exchanges and the sea channel, the road mainly to the South China Sea as the center, so called the South China Sea Silk Road. Maritime Silk Road formed in the Qin and Han Dynasties, the development of the Three Kingdoms to the Sui Dynasty, prosperity in the Tang and Song Dynasties, changed in the Ming Dynasty, is known as the most ancient maritime routes.

  • THE BELT AND ROAD CREATES NEW OPPORTUNITIES

      "The Belt and Road" strategy opportunities Opportunities for industrial innovation

      Industrial innovation involves industrial transformation and upgrading and industrial transfer and other dividends. With the implementation of the "The Belt and Road" strategy, some of China's quality surplus industries will be transferred to other countries and regions. In the country, because the market supply and demand changes, some surplus industries, perhaps in other countries can be just a reasonable valuation; in the country, because the factor costs rise in some industries, products lose the price competitiveness, perhaps in other countries, Low factor costs will make these industries reproduce. In the country, because the export of some developed countries affected the development of the entire industry, perhaps in other countries will be able to bypass these barriers, and so on. In addition, due to industrial transformation caused by industrial transformation and upgrading is an unlimited opportunity, such as technological transformation, R & D investment, brand tree, etc. will give investors unlimited opportunities.

      "The Belt and Road" strategy opportunities Opportunities for financial innovation

      "The Belt and Road" strategy implementation of the first need to have sufficient capital flow, a huge amount of financial needs can only be resolved through financial innovation. We have initiated the establishment of "ADB" and "Silk Road Fund", but this can only solve part of the financial problems, along the "belt" along the "road" countries and regions will be a variety of financial innovation, including the issue of various types Securities, the establishment of various types of funds and innovative financial mechanisms, etc., during which the dividends and opportunities as much as even unthinkable.

      "The Belt and Road" strategy opportunities Regional innovation opportunities

      "The Belt and Road" is essentially an international regional economic category, with the "The Belt and Road" strategy implementation, will lead to different countries and regions of regional innovation, including regional development model, regional industrial strategic choice, regional economy Technical path, cooperation between regions, etc., during which each innovation contains unlimited opportunities.

  • THE CONSTRUCTION OF THE BELT AND ROAD PRINCIPLE

    Building"The Belt and Road"construction is adhering to the principle of co-operation, sharing and co-construction, abide by the purposes and principles of the Charter of the United Nations and abide by the five principles of peaceful coexistence.

    Adhere to open cooperation

    "The Belt and Road" related countries based on but not limited to the scope of the ancient Silk Road, countries and international and regional organizations can participate in, so that the results of the benefits to a wider range of areas.

    Adhere to harmony and tolerance

    Advocate the tolerance of civilization, respect for the choice of national development path and mode, strengthen the dialogue between different civilizations, seek common ground while reserving differences, inclusive, peaceful coexistence, symbiosis and common prosperity.

    Adhere to the operation of the market

    Follow the rules of the market and international rules, give full play to the market in the allocation of resources in the decisive role and the role of various types of enterprises, while playing the role of the government.

    Adhere to mutual benefit and win-win situation

    Taking into account the interests and concerns of all parties, to seek the best fit and cooperation between the number of covenants, to reflect the wisdom and creativity of all parties, the director of the facilities, do their best to give full play to the advantages and potential of the parties.

  • THE MAIN MEANING OF THE BELT AND ROAD

    1. To explore the way of economic growth

    "The Belt and Road" is in the post-financial crisis era, as the world's economic growth locomotive China, its own production capacity, technology and financial advantages, experience and model advantages into market and cooperation advantages, the implementation of a comprehensive open innovation. Through the "The Belt and Road" to build China's reform and development dividends, China's development experience and lessons. China will focus on promoting cooperation and dialogue between countries along the line, the establishment of a more balanced and balanced new global development partnership, consolidate the long-term stable development of the world economy.

    2. Create a new type of cooperation

    China's reform and opening up is the largest innovation in the world today. With the development of economic corridor theory, economic belt theory and international cooperation theory in the 21st century, the theory of economic development, regional cooperation theory, globalization theory The "The Belt and Road" emphasizing the principle of sharing, building and sharing, beyond the Marshall Plan, foreign aid and going out strategy, to the 21st century international cooperation to bring new ideas.
